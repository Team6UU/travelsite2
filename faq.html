Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a package holiday?

Currently, our online booking portal is under development, but our contact form is open 24/7 and a staff member will be able to assist you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept most major credit/debit cards(Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and Paypal for package holidays.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Cancellation and modifications can vary depending on the package and service provider. Please refer to the provider, or our own support form for further assistance.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer various options for travel insurance that will fit your needs. You will be able to add travel insurance during the booking process or add it retroactively by contacting us.

When will I receive my holiday documents?

If you've entered an email address, you will usually receive them within 24 hours, and again 28 days before your holiday. If you've requested them via post then it may take up to 3 weeks to be delivered.

Do I need to provide my middle name for a booking?

No, we don't usually require your middle name when making a booking. However, it may be necessary if two people on the booking have an identical forename and surname. Please ensure it is spelt exactly as it is on your passport.

Question not answered? Still Unsure? Ask us and we will assist you as best as we can.
